# Example banking FAQ knowledge base for RAG
# Add more Q&A pairs as needed

What is a savings account?
A savings account is a deposit account held at a financial institution that provides principal security and a modest interest rate.

How can I check my account balance?
You can check your account balance via online banking, mobile app, ATM, or by visiting your branch.

What is UPI?
UPI (Unified Payments Interface) is a real-time payment system developed by NPCI for instant money transfers between banks in India.

How do I apply for a personal loan?
You can apply for a personal loan online, through mobile banking, or by visiting your nearest branch. Eligibility criteria apply.

What is an overdraft?
An overdraft is a credit facility that allows you to withdraw more money than your account balance, up to a specified limit.
